Daisy

Origin and Geographic Distribution

Daisy flowers are vibrant, cheerful, and easy to grow, making them a proven choice for gardens. Stunning, reliable bloomers, both in the garden and as cut flowers, daisies are hardy, drought-tolerant plants that provide years of gorgeous, classic charm. This star-shaped flowering plant can be either annual or a perennial and comes in a variety of colors from bright white to the most electrifying shades of pinks, purples, and yellows. Propagation and Planting
